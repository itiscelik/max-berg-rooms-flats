Muhammed Celik, Founder & CEO

Created in 2014 by Muhammed Celik, Max-Berg Rooms & Flats is now Turkey-wide. Our aim is to provide safe & cheap accommodation for exchange students during exchange periods in Istanbul based on a strong alumni community with full satisfaction.

We love what we do for exchange students in Istanbul. For over 7 years, we have been helping exchange students around the world to transform experience by finding effective accommodation with the use of friendship.
